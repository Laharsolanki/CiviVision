Urban areas face delays in resolving civic issues due to unorganized complaint systems, duplicate reports, and lack of real-time tracking.
Citizens often remain unaware of the status of their complaints. 
**CiviVision** solves this by:

- Providing real-time complaint tracking  
- Using AI to classify and prioritize issues

## Our Solution
**CiviVision** provides a digital platform where:
- Citizens can report civic issues with images and descriptions
- AI automatically categorizes reported issues
- Authorities receive structured and prioritized complaints
- Citizens can track progress in real time
